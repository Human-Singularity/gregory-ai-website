GregoryAI

Gregory is an Artifical Intelligence research assistant that constantly scouts trustworthy sources in any field, indexing and filtering the results into a searchable database.

View on GitHub

Cut through the noise

Gregory fetches scientific papers in real time and uses Machine Learning to parse which are most relevant. It extracts key points from abstracts, and categorizes papers based on keywords, saving your team weeks of literary research.

Stay on top of current scientific research

Configure Gregory to keep an eye out for specific key words or new avenues of research, and get a personalized weekly newsletter breaking down new exciting research on your field. Gregory also allows teams to customize newsletters for different stakeholders, either keeping information high level, or more granular.

Keep up with research trends

Gregory’s analytics dashboard allows teams to track in real time what research terms are becoming more popular. This way you’ll be able to track new treatments or information, and easily check what success rate other researches have described — and what you can add to the conversation.

Features focused on scientific papers

  • Fetch search results from PubMed and other science journals
  • Identify authors and their ORCID
  • Automatic emails to the admin team with results in the last 48hours
  • Configure email lists for different stakeholders
  • Configure categories to organize search results based on keywords in title
